Q: I don’t have an official English language certificate. Can I prove my English language skills any other way?”
A: Yes. Your knowledge of the English language is also proven in the following cases:1) You have completed at least 15 ECTS credits worth of English taught modules*. 2) Your bachelor’s thesis was written in English*. In this case, the bachelor’s thesis module should also span at least 15 ECTS credits.
