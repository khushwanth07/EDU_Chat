Q: Can I study part-time?
A: Our program is designed as a full-time course with a standard duration of four semesters. However, you have the flexibility to extend your studies up to six semesters if needed. To stay on track, you must meet the following milestones:
• At least 30 ECTS by the end of the 3rd semester
• 60 ECTS by the 4th semester
• 90 ECTS by the 5th semester
• 120 ECTS by the 6th semester
